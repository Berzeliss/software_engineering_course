# SkillMatch – Project Constitution

## 1. Vision

SkillMatch aims to become a transparent and intelligent job-matching platform that helps individuals find meaningful employment based on their skills, experience, and career goals. The long-term vision is to reduce the gap between job seekers and employers by making hiring more efficient, fair, and data-driven.

---

## 2. Mission

The mission of SkillMatch is to design a system that accurately connects job seekers with relevant job opportunities by analyzing their skills, experience, and preferences. The platform focuses on improving hiring efficiency for companies while supporting users in finding suitable and fulfilling job opportunities.

---

## 3. Core Values

- **Fairness**: Every user should have equal access to job opportunities regardless of background.
- **Transparency**: Matching decisions should be understandable and explainable.
- **Privacy**: User data must be protected and handled securely at all times.
- **Efficiency**: The system should reduce time spent searching and applying for jobs.
- **User-Centric Design**: The needs of job seekers and companies are at the center of all decisions.

---

## 4. Guiding Principles

- All requirements must be clearly defined, testable, and traceable throughout development.
- The system should prioritize explainable matching rather than black-box decisions.
- User data must always be stored and transmitted securely.
- Each feature must provide measurable value to either job seekers or employers.
- The system should be designed in iterative steps, ensuring continuous improvement.
- Performance and scalability must be considered in every design decision.

---

## 5. Stakeholders

- **Job Seekers**: Individuals searching for job opportunities that match their skills and preferences.
- **Employers / Companies**: Organizations posting job listings and searching for suitable candidates.
- **System Administrators**: Responsible for maintaining system stability, security, and performance.
- **Developers / Designers**: Responsible for designing, implementing, and improving the system.
